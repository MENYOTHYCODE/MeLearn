### Add Your First Task
1. Click **Study Planner** in sidebar
2. Click "Add Task" button
3. Fill in title and subject
4. Click "Add Task"

### Create Your First Note
1. Click **Notes** in sidebar
2. Click "New Note" button
3. Write your note
4. Choose a color
5. Click "Create Note"

### Start a Study Session
1. Click **Study Room** in sidebar
2. Set work time (default: 25 min)
3. Click "Start"
4. Focus on your studies!

### Search for Resources
1. Click **Resources** in sidebar
2. Choose a tab (Books/Videos/Topics/Dictionary)
3. Enter search term
4. Click "Search"
